About Mez

Mez was born and raised in North Dakota to Eritrean parents and has always had a love for telling stories. As a graduate of Tisch School of the Arts at NYU he continues to build on over 10 years of filmmaking experience. From writing to producing, set production to post production, Mez continues to expand his knowledge to becoming a more complete storyteller. The next chapter for Mez is building on that experience and incorporating programming languages and practices to make the production process from start to finish better and more efficient. Currently on track to becoming a full stack developer, Mez hopes to incorporate his passion for storytelling into a new field.
